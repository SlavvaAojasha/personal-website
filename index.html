About the Artist

Slavva Aojasha is a tattoo designer specializing in cyber-sigilism — merging the esoteric and the algorithmic. Each piece is a ritual between code and flesh, meticulously drawn for seekers of digital sanctity.

  • Name: Rafika Puteri Ayu Nirmala
  • Alias: Slavva Aojasha
  • Based in: Surabaya, Indonesia
  • Specialties: Cyber-Sigilism, Abstract, Fine-Line Geometry
  • Experience: 2+ years in custom tattoo design and body symbology
  • Mediums Beyond Skin: Digital Illustration, Tattoo Concept Design
